It's time to work on what matters. Stoicard is here to help.

The Daily Card

Stoicard is based on the idea that today my be the last day of your life. It presents you with a single card for the day to focus on your goals and ensure that your time in invested well.

Focus Blocks

Each card gives you a group of focus blocks. These are time allocations you can dedicated to work that matters. You may choose to invest one in your family and the other two in learning a new skill. It's up to you.

Countdown

At the top of your daily card is a countdown timer to midnight. It's there to remind you that your time is a gift and should never be wasted. Manage your day by looking at the amount of time you have left compared to the focus commitments you've made for yourself.

To-Not Tracker

Your day is full of needless to-do items that make little differece to achieving your life's goals. Every time you come across one of these tasks, add it to your to-not tracker as a reminder of what you are not willing to waste time on.

Reflection

The stoics found value in reflecting on how they invested their time at the end of each day. They saw it as a chance to wrap up thie affairs incase it was their last day to live. If they woke up in th emorning, they were grateful for a new gift of 24 hours and they planned to make the most of it.

Archived Cards

See the progress you've made in investing your time well. Stoicard archives each of your daily cards into a mini journal. As you look back on how you have lived, where can you make improvements to get the most out of today?
