A Stoic Approach to Living Well

It Will End

Stoic philosophers like Seneca and Epictetus taught that we should meditate on our own death. It may sound gruesome, but it's a valuable tool. Doing so helps you realize that your time on earth is precious.

Life is a Gift

What will your life mean? When you look back on what you've accomplished, wht will you see? Take time to design the life you want, and then work each day to make it a reality.

Don't Waste Time

Time is ticking. Havnpe you ever wondered how successful people achieve so much? It's becuase they value time. Time is not to be wasted. It's to be invested.
